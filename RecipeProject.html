Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Puree
  • Egg
  • Onion & Garli

Recipe

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

Paneer Shwarma

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Wheat Roti
  • Masalas
  • Mayonnaise & Sauces
  • Onion & Capsicum- Chopped

Recipe

  1. Take paneer and mash it with a potato masher
  2. Add this mixture and roast it from both the sides.
  3. Then, make chapatis by using wheat flour.
  4. Spread mayonnaise on top of the bread and then add the paneer mixture.
  5. Serve along with mint chutney and mayonnaise.
